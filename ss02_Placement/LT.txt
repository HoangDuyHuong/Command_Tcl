PLACEMENT 
Tong quan 5 buoc trong qua trinh Placement 
I. Placement khong phai dat tat ca cac cong logic vao ban do chip cung mot luc ma cong cu se thuc hien qua 5 cong doan chinh
1. Global Placement: Sap vi tri so bo ban dau cho cac cell, tap trung toi uu hoa tong chieu dau dd (wirelenght) va congestion.
2. optimization: Toi uu hoa 1 lan bang thay doi kich thuoc cell (resize cell), move hoac chen them bo dem (buffer)
3. Legalize Placement: Dua cac cell ve vi tri hop le (on standard row, no overlap,...)
4. Detailed Placement: Tinh chinh vi mo de sua loi timing/congestion 
5. Final optimization: sua dut diem cac loi vi pham tren cac duong truyen tin hieu quan trong.

II. Cac khai niem cot loi ve timing & parasitics (ky sinh)
   Trong thuc te tin hieu khong phai la cac xung vuong hoan hao. Delay cua tin hieu phu thuoc vao dien tro (R) va dien dung (C) cua cong logic lan day doan- Transition delay: La time de tin hieu chuyen trang thai tu 0->1 hoac tu 1->0
- Delay tren day (Net RC): L dd cang dai thi R va C cang lon, Day cang rong (W) thi R giam nhung C lai tang, RC cang lon, tin hieu cang cham.
- Coupling Capacitance (dien dung ro ri cheo): Say ra khi 2 dd chay // cung mot luc, hai dd dat cang sat nhau hoac duong chay // cang dai thi dien dung nhieu cang cao -> tanng delay.
- Fanout & output Capacitance: Fanout la SL cell tai (load) ma 1 cell co the lai (driver) phai keo, Fanout lon lam tang dien dung tai, dan den delay cang cao.
- Driver Strength (Suc keo cua cell): phu thuoc vao ti le W/L cua transistor ben trong cell, ty le cang lon -> cell cang to -> suc keo cang khoe -> giam delay.
- Pre-route va post-route: buoc Placement chua cos day kim loai that, nen RC chi la uoc tinh, phai den buoc routing, duong day moi co that va RC moi dc trich xuat chinh xac.

III. Cac tip de fix timing/congestion
* Toi uu chieu dai day (Optimize wirelenght): Co the dua cell lai va cell tai lai gan nhau, hoac chen them buffer vao duong day de chia nho RC.
* Remove Buffer: neu duong day ngan nhung lai di qua nhieu buffer, viec xoa bot buffer se giup triet tieu bot do tre cong don cua cac cong (cell delay).
* Split Fanout: Thay vi de 1 cell ganh 3 tai cell o xa, chen them buffer de re nhanh, giup giam ganh nang ve dien dung va rut ngan day.
* Up/Down sizecell: co the doi tu cell nho sang cell lon de tang suc keo (driver strength), giup tin hieu chay nhanh hon. doi lai cell to se ton dien tich va cong suat.
* Toi uu dien tich: cos the gop nhieu logic co ban thanh cac cong phuc hop de giam SL cell va area. Tuy nhien, can than vi cpmplexcell co the co nhieu pin co the gay congestion.
* Fix congestion: Doi cac cell ra khoi khu vuc qua chat choi va cho phep thuat toan di day qua lai.

IV. Legalization, Placement Status & Blockage
* Legal Placement: Cell phai nam dung tren cac row, khop ngam voi cac thanh nguon PG (VDD/VSS) va tuyet doi khong duoc overlap, neu vi pham, chip co the bi chap mach va hong khi SX.
* 5 trang thai cua cell:
- Unplaced: co trong netlist nhung chua duoc tha cao ban do.
- Placed: Da nam hop le, tool co the tu do di chuyen.
- Fixed: Bi khoa chat vi tri, tool khong the doi di, ng co the doi di (thuong dung cho Macros, Pad).
- Cover: Bi khoa tuyet doi, ca tool lan ky su deu khong duoc phep di chuyen hay doi kich thuoc.
- Soft Fixed: Khong duoc di chuyen o buoc global, nhung duoc phep xe dich nhe o buoc optimize, khoang cach move limit.

* Placement Blockage:
- Hard Blockage: Cam dat bat ky standard cell vao vung nay.
- Soft Blockage: chi cho phep dat Buffer va Inveter de noi day di xuyen qua.
- Partial Blockage: Cho phep dat moi loai cell nhung gioi han mat do (chi dc lap day toi da 40% dien tich vung cam).

V. Luong thuc hien Placement
1. Global Placement: tool tu dong dat cac standard cell vao ban do chip. O buoc nay tool ch quan tam cell co nam dung row hay overlap hay ko, muc tieu chinh la lam sao toi uu wirelenght, congestion, dap ung timing co ban.
2. Optimize: Giai quyet cac van de timing xau (do wirelenght dai hoac Fanout qua lon), cac thao tac toi uu bao gom: resize cell, move cell, add buffer, Fanout Split.
3. Legalize Placement: Dua cac cell sau khi da toi uu vao dung row, fix overlap.
4. Detail Placement & Final Optimize: Legalize cell co the lam timing te di, congestion. O buoc nay se tinh chinh vi mo (doi cho, move nhe), va opt lan cuoi de chot timing va sua cac loi cuc bo.

* Muc tieu: 
- Function equivalent: Mach sau khi toi uu,add buffer, change logic gate, van phai co chuc nang tuong duong voi RTL design ban dau.
- Toi uu hieu xuat: Giam thieu toi da khaong cach giua cac logic gate de tang toc do truyen tai tin hieu cua chip.
- Kiem soat Uti: Trong qua trinh toi uu timing (add  Buff, tang sizecell), dien tich tong cua cac cell se tang len khoang 5-10%, vi du su gia tang mat do theo tung khau: init (50%) -> after PlaceOpt (60%) -> after CTS (62-64%), after routing (67-69%). Ky su phai nam ro dieu nay de du tru dien tich, ko lam chip bi qua tai.

VI. Hai khai niem timing cot loi
* SetUp Time (T_su):
  - Dinh nghia: La khoang time toi thieu ma data phai on dinh (stable) trc khi co suon xun nhip (Clock trigger)
  - Ex: Giong nhu viec ban phai co mat tai ben xe buyt 15 phut truoc gio xe chay de kip checkin, cat hanh ly, on dinh cho ngoi.
  - Violation: Data den qua tre, truot mat nhip clock.
* Hold Time (T_hd): 
  - Dinh nghia: la khoang thoi gian toi thieu ma tin hieu phai tiep tuc on dinh sau khi co suon xung nhip.
  Ex: Giong nhu ban buoc len tau dien ngam, ban phai dung yen trong do mot luc cho den khi cua dong lai, neu vua buoc vao da ngay lap tuc buoc ra truoc khicua dong, he thong se bi nhieu va ko ghi nhan ban da len tau.
  - Violation: Data thay doi qua nhanh ngay khi clock vua danh.

VII. Tom tat cac ky thuat fix timing
- Fix SetUp Time: focus on viec tin hieu di nhanh hon, cong cu se tang kich thuoc cell lai (Size up cell), fix Transition, move cell lai gan hon de rut ngan duong truyen.
- Fix Hold Time: focus on viec la tin hieu di cham lai de khong Violation T_hd. Tool se chen them buffer vao data Path de cau gio, dam bao du lieu khong bi thay doi trang thai qua som.

VIII. Cach fix timing cu the
1. Khac phuc violate Setup timing
- Dinh nghia: Setup time a khoang thoi thieu "truoc" suon xung clock ma tin hieu phai stable 
- Viloate: Xay ra khi du lieu den qua cham so voi mua thoi gian yeu cau, tham chi co the sau khi clock active.
- Nguyen tac fix: Bat buoc phai lam giam do tre cua data path va delay on standard cell.
- Cac phuong phap thuc hien:
  * Size up cell de tang suc keo (driver strength), giup tin hieu di nhanh hon.
  * Add buffer vao cac duong day qua dai -> cat nho do tre cua day dan.
  * Doi cac cell tren duong truyen lai gan nhau de ngan vi tri vat ly, giam delay tren day
  * Optimize logic on data path.

2. Khac phuc violate hold timing
- Dinh nghia: Hold time la khoang thoi gian toi thieu "sau" suong xung nhip ma tin hieu phai tiep tuc giu trang thai on dinh de bat dung nhip 
- Violate: Xay ra khi data truyen den qua nhanh, neu thay doi trang thai trong hold time -> ghi de sai data.
- Nguyen tac fix: Trai nguoc voi set up, phai tang do tre tren data path va standard cell.
- Cac phuong phap thuc hien:
  * Size down cell de lam yeu suc keo -> tin hieu truyen cham hon.;
  * Add 1 or more buffer de co tinh tao them do delay cho duong truyen, nhung phai tinh toan de khong violate Setup timing.
  * Ve duong day vong veo (detour net) de keo dai khoang cach vat ly, tang thoi gian truyen tin hieu.
  * Neu truong hop re nhanh ma violate hold time (nhanh in put A & B noi va out nhanh C), thay vi add buffer o out put (trc nhanh C) se anh huong den timing cua nhanh A->C, ta phai add buffer o nhanh B trc diem noi.
